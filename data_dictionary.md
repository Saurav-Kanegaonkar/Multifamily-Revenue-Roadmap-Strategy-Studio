# Data Dictionary

| Table | Grain | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| `data/customer_feedback.csv` | customer evidence record | Captures persona, source, theme, urgency, pain score, and linked product opportunity |
| `data/product_usage.csv` | opportunity by day | Captures adoption, override, explainability, approval, export, and data freshness telemetry |
| `analysis/outputs/roadmap_priority.csv` | product opportunity | Scores each roadmap bet by customer pull, revenue impact, strategy fit, confidence, effort, and risk |
| `analysis/outputs/launch_plan.csv` | launch gate | Defines concept-to-GA gates, owners, success metrics, and readiness status |
| `analysis/outputs/app_payload.json` | application payload | Powers the static strategy studio UI |

All datasets are synthetic and are generated by `scripts/score_operating_data.py`.
